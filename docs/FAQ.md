# Public Repository FAQ

## Why is this repository public while the core product is private?

This repository is intended to be the public-facing surface for Vartovii. It
gives reviewers, partners, and contributors a clean view of product positioning,
architecture, roadmap direction, and integration shape without exposing
protected implementation details.

## Does this repository contain the full application?

No. It does not include the private backend, internal data pipelines, deployment
configuration, or protected scoring infrastructure used in production.

## What does Vartovii actually deliver?

Vartovii delivers machine-verifiable trust outputs through dashboards, reports,
and API-ready response shapes. The product is designed for Web3 risk
intelligence and corporate due diligence workflows.

## Is the trust score meant to replace expert review?

No. Vartovii is decision-support infrastructure. It improves signal quality,
evidence attribution, and workflow repeatability, but it does not replace legal,
investment, compliance, or employment review.

## Why are screenshots and examples included here?

They provide an accurate public view of the product surface without requiring
access to internal systems or protected code.
